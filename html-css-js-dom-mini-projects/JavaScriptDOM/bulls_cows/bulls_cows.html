Welcome to Bulls and Cows game!

Bulls and Cows is a classic code-breaking game where you try to guess a secret code. In this game, the computer will generate a random 4-digit number, and your goal is to guess the number in as few attempts as possible.

A bull bull means you have guessed a digit correctly and it is in the right position.
A cow means you have guessed a digit correctly, but it is in the wrong position.

Good luck and have fun playing!
